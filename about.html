About Me

When People Succeed and Designs Come To Life

Those are the moments I strive for. With a background as a figure skating coach and as a commercial interior designer, I consider it my mission to design for specific needs. The “human-centered” design is what drew me into User Experience (UX) Design.

From a very young age I designed with a purpose, continually taking in physical and psychological information about users or personas. I consider how they might complete a skill or respond in a certain situation, and I adapt my approach for the desired outcome.

My passion is to teach others. I lead by example and inspire those around me to keep learning and creating. I believe in recognizing the unique strengths of individuals, so they can achieve their goals.

Want to see what else I've done?

I designed the interior of a showroom/office/warehouse for acoustical ceiling products. The owner wanted to showcase a wide variety of the ceiling products that his business was well known for installing.

My challenge as the interior designer was to learn the capabilities and requirements for over 20 types of specialty ceilings, incorporate them with the lighting systems, and to design them into these new spaces. The business owner could then take clients through the space to show off the products. I worked directly with the business owner and the architect in order to stay within branding and product requirements.
